      Why do birthdays follow patterns?
      What's the best and worst score possible?

      Why is Tuesday permanently unlucky?

      Each milestone age has a fixed day-shift range. For example, 16 years always contains 3-5 leap years, so your 16th birthday always shifts +5 or +6 days from your birth day. From Tuesday, that means Sunday or Monday — never Friday or Saturday.

      Of the 12 milestones, only two have shifts that can reach Friday or Saturday from Tuesday: the 30th (+2 or +3 days → Thu/Fri) and the 70th (+3 or +4 days → Fri/Sat). The other 10 milestones are mathematically locked out, which is why Tuesday can never score higher than 2.

      Thursday and Sunday aren't "luckier" by chance — their starting positions simply align better with the fixed shifts that these specific milestone ages produce.
